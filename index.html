If you are a Year 13 student attempting to create a website inline with the AS91903 NCEA standard, or a student about to attempt the AS91903 standard who wants to develop a website, then this website will help explain the standard.

This website will focus on explaining key word(s) in the standard, and what they mean in terms of developing a website. In particular, this website is focused on relevant implications and complex techniques, with some explanations of other wording, for development of a website. It does not tell you how to develop a website, but how to make your website as good as possible in terms of the AS91903 (digital media) standard.
