AS91903 Standard Explanations

Hey there! This is a website all about the NCEA AS91903 Standard

This will include explanations of particular key terms, as well as information on the various examples provided in the standard. These are all being explained in relation to developing a website. It will include the following:

  • An explanation of the relevant implications
  • An explanation of the complex techniques
  • An explanation of "user experience principles", "testing procedures", "data integrity" and "end user requirements"
